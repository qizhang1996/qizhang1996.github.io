Biography

I am currently serving as an Associate Professor at the School of Computer and Communication Engineering, University of Science and Technology Beijing, China. Prior to this appointment, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow (2023–2025) at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Ronghua Li. Earlier, I received my PhD degree from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Guoren Wang, with Prof. RongHua Li as co-supervisor.

My general research interest lies in graph data management, particularly cohesive subgraph search and graph algorithms.
